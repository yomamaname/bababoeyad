## Ingredients

- 2 pounds russet potatoes
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 cup flour, plus more as needed
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 5 tablespoons butter
- 10 sage leaves

## Method

1. Bake potatoes until tender, then rice the flesh while warm.
2. Mix with egg yolk, salt, and just enough flour to form a soft dough.
3. Roll into ropes, cut into pillows, and mark with a fork if desired.
4. Boil until the gnocchi float.
5. Toss in browned butter with crisp sage leaves.

## Texture tip

Use a light touch. The less you knead, the more cloud-like the gnocchi will be.
