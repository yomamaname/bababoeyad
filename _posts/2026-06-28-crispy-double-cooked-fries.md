## Ingredients

- 3 large russet potatoes
- Neutral frying oil
- Fine salt
- Optional: garlic aioli, ketchup, or malt vinegar

## Method

1. Cut potatoes into batons and soak in cold water for 30 minutes.
2. Dry thoroughly, then fry at 325°F / 163°C until pale and flexible.
3. Rest for 10 minutes while the oil rises to 375°F / 190°C.
4. Fry again until deep golden and crisp.
5. Salt immediately and serve hot.

## Serving idea

Pile the fries high and add little bowls of aioli, smoky ketchup, and herbed mayonnaise so every bite can take a different route.
