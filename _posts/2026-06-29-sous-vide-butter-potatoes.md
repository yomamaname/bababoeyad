## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds baby Yukon Gold potatoes, halved if large
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 smashed garlic cloves
- 2 rosemary sprigs
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 tablespoon olive oil for finishing

## Method

1. Heat the sous vide bath to 194°F / 90°C.
2. Seal potatoes, butter, garlic, rosemary, and salt in a vacuum bag or sturdy freezer bag.
3. Cook for 60 minutes, until the potatoes are fully tender.
4. Drain, pat dry, and sear cut-side down in olive oil until golden.
5. Spoon over any buttery bag juices and finish with flaky salt.

## Why it works

Sous vide cooking gives every potato the same creamy texture from edge to center. The quick sear adds the roasted flavor and color people expect from a great potato side.
