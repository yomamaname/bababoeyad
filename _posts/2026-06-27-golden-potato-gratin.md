## Ingredients

- 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, thinly sliced
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
- 2 garlic cloves, grated
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1 tablespoon thyme leaves
- 1 cup grated Gruyère

## Method

1. Warm cream with garlic, salt, pepper, and thyme.
2. Layer potatoes in a buttered baking dish, scattering cheese between layers.
3. Pour the seasoned cream over the top.
4. Bake covered at 375°F / 190°C for 45 minutes.
5. Uncover and bake 20 to 30 minutes more, until bubbling and bronzed.

## Make it shine

Let the gratin rest for 15 minutes before serving so the cream settles into sliceable, glossy layers.
